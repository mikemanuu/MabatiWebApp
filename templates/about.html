Our Story

Our story began with a vision to revolutionize roofing industry by providing top-quality decra and mabati products that combine durability, affordability and style. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name, empowering communities to build homes and businesses that stand the test of time. Every step of the way, we have remained committed to innovation, exceptional customer service and sustainable practices..

  • Established with the goal of making durable roofing accessible to everyone.
  • Expanded operations to serve individuals, contactors and retailers across Kenya and beyond.
  • Recognized for our consistent quality and commitment to environmental responsibility.
